01 // Theme Narrative

Elevated minimalism for the modern curator.

The Noir About Me Theme is a magazine-inspired static page code. It was designed to bridge the gap between K-pop fan culture and high-fashion aesthetics. By utilizing a grid-based hierarchy and a strictly monochromatic palette, your content remains the focus, framed by a timeless editorial structure.
